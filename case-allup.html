Building Operational Systems That Scale

Early-stage startup building AI-powered job distribution infrastructure across the United States.

At allUP, I designed and owned the end-to-end operational system that supported the company's growth as it scaled across clients, roles, and geographies.

I translated leadership decisions into execution in high-urgency, multi–time zone environments, building the operational infrastructure required to grow without increasing complexity.

What I did

  • Designed and owned the operational flow from contract close to job launch
  • Replaced fragile, manual workflows with scalable, automated processes using no-code tools and AI
  • Established the weekly operating cadence, performance tracking, and issue resolution routines
  • Built and owned the performance analysis layer across the platform and LinkedIn

Impact

  • Scaled job distribution from ~20 to 1,500+ live roles across the U.S.
  • Enabled growth from a handful of clients to hundreds without proportional team growth
  • Created full visibility into role performance, geographic demand, and conversion rates
  • Supported faster, data-informed operational and distribution decisions
