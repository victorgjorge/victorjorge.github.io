Ribeirão Preto City Hall

Director of Innovation & Economic Development

Chief of Staff in a Complex Public-Sector Organization

Municipal government serving 700,000+ residents in one of Brazil's most economically dynamic regions.

In my role at Ribeirão Preto City Hall, I operated in a Chief of Staff–like capacity, working closely with executive leadership to translate strategy into execution across a highly complex, multi-stakeholder environment.

I acted as a generalist and strategic thought partner, supporting leadership by establishing the foundational systems required for large-scale execution, from legal frameworks and operating cadence to cross-department coordination and performance metrics.

What I did

  • Led the "Empresa Fácil" program end to end (legal + technology + operations)
  • Designed the legal framework classifying 1,067 low-risk business activities
  • Coordinated system integrations that reduced viability analysis time by 99.9% (to ~3 minutes)
  • Enabled instant business licensing for low-risk activities
  • Recruited and led teams using OKRs and agile operating rituals
